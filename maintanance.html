DALLOS BATHWARE CARE & MAINTENANCE POLICY

To ensure your Dallos Bathware products perform optimally and offer long-lasting durability, please follow these care and maintenance guidelines. Damage resulting from improper maintenance is not covered under warranty.

General Cleaning Guidelines:

1. Routine Cleaning: Use warm water, mild soap, and a soft cloth for daily cleaning.
2. Avoid Harsh Chemicals: Do not use abrasive cleaners, acidic solutions (like vinegar or hydrochloric acid), bleaches, ammonia-based products, or solvents. These can damage product finishes.
3. No Abrasive Tools: Avoid scouring pads, bristle brushes, wire wool, or emery paper. Use only soft sponges or cloths.
4. Spray Cleaners: Spray cleaning agents onto a cloth first, not directly onto the product, to prevent damage from seepage into openings.
5. Test First: Always test any cleaning product on a small, inconspicuous area before applying it to the entire surface.
6. Rinse and Dry: Rinse thoroughly with clean water after cleaning and dry with a soft cloth to prevent water deposits or limescale build-up.

Special Care Instructions:

1. Dry After Use: Blot dry any water to avoid deposits. Do not rub; gently dab.
2. Limescale Removal: Use lemon juice (not concentrate) or white vinegar, applied gently and rinsed thoroughly.
3. Toilet Cleaners: Use cautiously. Restrict use to inside the bowl only and rinse immediately if in contact with external surfaces.

Preventive Measures:

1. Water Scaling: Regularly clean to prevent mineral build-up, especially in edges and corners. Ensure water hardness does not exceed 300-400 ppm.
2. Acid Fumes: Avoid prolonged exposure to acid fumes from bathroom cleaners, which can react with brass components.
3. Coastal Care: In salty air environments, clean frequently to prevent corrosion.
4. Avoid Scratches: Use soft cloths only; avoid coarse materials or powders.

Additional Tips:

1. Avoid Mixing Cleaners: Different chemicals may react and harm surfaces.
2. Check Regularly: Inspect for any signs of damage, especially before and after installation.
3. Handle with Care: Ensure careful handling during installation and use
By following these guidelines, you can maintain the beauty and functionality of your Dallos Bathware products for years to come.

Customer Guidelines


1. To ensure adequate water pressure, it is advisable to keep the pipelines straight with minimum number of bends, which will support smooth flow of water.
2. All the supply lines must have vent pipes. These vent pipes help in removing air-locking in the water supply to give a smooth flow of water from the fittings.
3. Prior to plastering or fixing tiles, test the working of all the installed concealed fittings at high pressure to check for leakage, if any.
4. Place the outlet point in the overhead tank at least 25 cm above the ground level of the tank. This prevents the flow of foreign particles, sand etc., along with water supply into the fittings and thereby prevents possible damage to the working parts. Clean the tank thoroughly before starting the supply. It is suggested that the overhead tank must be cleaned at regular intervals to avoid inflow of foreign debris/particles.
5. To protect the fine finish of fittings from dirt, stains and other damages during construction, cover them with cloth or polythene after their installation, till they come into actual use.
